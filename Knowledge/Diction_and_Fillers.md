# Diction, Fillers, and Readability

## Common English fillers
um, uh, erm, like, you know

## Common Filipino conversational fillers
ano, parang, alam mo, kumbaga

Fillers can be meaningful in a verbatim transcript. In a polished document, repeated fillers may be removed after review. Vague words such as “thing,” “stuff,” “bagay,” or “ganun” may need a more specific noun when context permits.
