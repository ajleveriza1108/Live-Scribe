# English Grammar Review Notes

This compact reference is designed for post-session comments, not automatic rewriting.

## High-confidence checks
- Subject–verb agreement: “I am,” “he/she doesn’t,” “they were.”
- Double comparative: prefer either “better” or “more effective,” not “more better.”
- Double superlative: prefer either “best” or “most effective,” not “most best.”
- Sentence boundaries: long spoken runs may need punctuation in a readable document.

## Caution
Speech can be informal, quoted, dialectal, humorous, or intentionally ungrammatical. Preserve the transcript and place improvements only in the comments section.
