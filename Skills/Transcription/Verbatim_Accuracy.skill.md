# Verbatim Accuracy Skill

## Purpose
Preserve what the speaker actually said. Do not translate, summarize, improve ideas, or silently fix grammar during transcription.

## Rules
- Keep English, Tagalog, Filipino, and Taglish code-switching as spoken.
- Treat names, numbers, dates, money, addresses, Bible references, product names, and technical terms as high-risk details.
- Prefer an uncertainty comment over inventing a word.
- Use the complete saved WAV for the final accuracy pass.
- Keep the live transcript separate from the final reviewed transcript.

## ASR hotwords
- Tagalog
- Filipino
- Taglish
- Pilipinas
- Metro Manila
- barangay
