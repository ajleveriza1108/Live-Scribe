# Taglish Preservation Skill

## Purpose
Recognize natural Filipino code-switching without forcing the entire phrase into one language.

## Rules
- Preserve English words inside Tagalog sentences and Tagalog words inside English sentences.
- Do not translate common particles such as po, opo, naman, kasi, pala, nga, and daw.
- Preserve respectful forms and conversational markers when a verbatim transcript is required.
- Capitalize proper names only during the final document pass.
