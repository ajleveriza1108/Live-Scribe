# Grammar Comments Skill

## Purpose
Create review comments after the speaker is finished without secretly rewriting the source transcript.

## Rules
- Put the original wording, suggested wording, and a brief explanation in separate fields.
- Label uncertain rules as suggestions.
- Do not treat normal Taglish code-switching as automatically incorrect.
- Do not correct quoted speech when exact wording is important.
- Prioritize obvious agreement, tense, repeated-comparative, and sentence-boundary issues.
