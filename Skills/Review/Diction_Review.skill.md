# Diction Review Skill

## Purpose
Help a speaker improve clarity after transcription.

## Rules
- Flag repeated filler words, vague nouns, accidental word repetition, and unusually long spoken sentences.
- Keep fillers in the verbatim transcript; only suggest removing them from a polished copy.
- Diction comments describe wording and clarity. They do not claim to diagnose pronunciation or a speech condition.
- Recommend replaying the WAV when a possible repetition may have come from the ASR engine.
