# Live Scribe Language Test Videos

These links are intended only for functional testing of **Computer audio / livestream** mode.
The videos remain hosted by their respective YouTube creators. Links can change or be removed.

## How to test

1. Download one speech quality option inside Live Scribe.
2. Set **Listen to** to **Computer audio / livestream**.
3. Click **Detect** and choose the speakers or headphones playing YouTube.
4. Select the matching language in **Settings**.
5. Play one video and click **Start Listening**.
6. Test approximately two to five minutes of clear speech.
7. Click **Stop & Save WAV**, then **Verify from WAV**.
8. Compare the result with the video's subtitles when available.

## What to record during testing

For each language, note:

- Speech quality selected
- CPU or NVIDIA GPU mode
- Live transcription delay
- Words missed in the live pass
- Words corrected by Verify from WAV
- Names, numbers, and punctuation errors
- Whether the saved WAV contains the full test
- Any issue with DOCX, TXT, or SRT export

Do not claim a language is fully tested until a fluent or native speaker reviews several real recordings.
